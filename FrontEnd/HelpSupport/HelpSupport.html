Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started with HealthQuest?

Simply sign up for a free account and complete the onboarding process. We'll guide you through setting up your profile and preferences.

How does the XP system work?

Earn XP by completing daily quests, logging meals, and maintaining healthy habits. Level up to unlock new features and achievements.

Can I connect with friends?

Yes! Use the Buddy Mode feature to add friends, compete on the leaderboard, and stay motivated together.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information and health data.
